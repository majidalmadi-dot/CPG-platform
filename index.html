National CPG Authority — Governance Architecture
Multi-tiered authority model aligned with WHO, NICE, and GIN-McMaster governance standards
TIER 1 — National Steering Committee (NSC)Strategic oversight · Priority setting · Final endorsement · Royal mandate
TIER 2 — Technical Oversight Committee (TOC)Methodological standards · Framework compliance · Quality assurance · COI
TIER 3 — Guideline Development Groups (GDGs)Topic-specific panels · Evidence synthesis · GRADE appraisal · Recommendations
TIER 4 — Evidence Review Teams (ERTs)Systematic reviews · Data extraction · Risk of bias · GRADE profiling
TIER 5 — External Review & Public ConsultationPeer review · Stakeholder feedback · Patient input · International experts

🤖 What This Skill Does
Stage 1 — Scoping: Builds PICO table, defines eligibility criteria, generates scope document with Saudi/Gulf context.
Stage 2 — Search: Constructs multi-database search strategies, validates with PRESS checklist, tracks PRISMA flow.
Stage 3 — Appraisal: Applies RoB 2/ROBINS-I/NOS tools per study design, builds GRADE evidence profiles.
Stage 4 — Synthesis: Creates Summary of Findings tables, narrative synthesis, effect estimates.
Stage 5 — EtR: Completes 11-domain EtR framework, drafts recommendations with strength + certainty, Delphi integration.

AI Skill: Systematic Review & Meta-Analysis
PRISMA 2020 pipeline — protocol to publication
Active
Stage 0–1: Protocol
PICO/PICOS/SPIDER formulation, PROSPERO registration, PRISMA-P checklist, eligibility criteria, statistical plan
Stage 2–3: Search & Screen
Multi-database strategy (PubMed, Embase, Cochrane, CINAHL), PRESS checklist, dual screening, PRISMA flow diagram
Stage 4: Risk of Bias
Auto-selects tool by design: RoB 2 (RCTs), ROBINS-I (NRS), NOS (cohort/case-control), QUADAS-2 (diagnostic), AMSTAR 2 (SRs)
Stage 5: Meta-Analysis
Fixed/random effects, Mantel-Haenszel / inverse variance, I² heterogeneity, Cochran Q, τ², prediction intervals
Stage 5b: Plots & Bias
Forest plots, funnel plots, Egger test, Begg test, trim-and-fill, subgroup analysis, sensitivity (leave-one-out)
Stage 6: GRADE & Write-up
GRADE certainty per outcome, Summary of Findings table, full PRISMA 2020 manuscript with all sections
